The Shop

Things I love & things I make

Curated with intention

Everything here is something I use, believe in, or have made myself. Nothing is here just to fill space. Slow shopping for a slower life.

A note on how this works

I only collaborate with brands and products I genuinely use and believe in. I do not take paid placements, accept gifted reviews without disclosure, or recommend things I haven't personally tested. When something is a collaboration, I say so clearly. When I earn a commission through a recommendation, I say so clearly. That's the deal.
